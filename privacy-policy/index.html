Data we collect

We may collect your email address, company information, role, inquiry details, and other information you choose to submit through the website.

How we use data

We use your data to manage setup requests, respond to inquiries, provide commercial communication related to the requested service, support account or billing setup, and maintain the security and reliability of the website.

Processors

Form submissions are processed by Formspree. Hosting is provided via Cloudflare Pages. If billing is enabled, payment-related personal data may also be processed by Paddle as merchant of record.

Cross-border transfers

Data may be processed in countries other than your country of residence, including where our service providers operate. We rely on appropriate safeguards provided by these processors.

Retention

We retain setup-request data only for as long as necessary to manage onboarding, support, and service evaluation or until you request deletion, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data and withdraw consent at any time. Contact us using the details above.
