Pricing

Commercial structure for AdsChecks verification plans.

Billing unit

AdsChecks bills by verified page. A verified page means one completed page load for a target URL in a selected check context (GEO + device), with verification of up to 5 predefined ad slots.

Plan structure

  • Free Trial - $0, limited to 1 run per day, 1 page per run, and 1 slot per page.
  • Starter - $39/month, up to 500 verified pages per month.
  • Standard - $99/month, up to 2,000 verified pages per month.
  • Custom - contact sales for higher-volume usage.

Free Trial does not convert to a paid plan automatically.

What is included

  • slot statuses for up to 5 predefined ad slots per verified page
  • screenshots and JSON metadata
  • UTC timestamp and check context (GEO/device)
  • evidence references for review and dispute handling

Charging rules

Usage is charged when a page load is completed.

Charged outcomes: OK, SLOT_EMPTY, CHALLENGE_DETECTED, BLOCKED.

When monthly plan capacity is reached, AdsChecks uses a hard cap. New runs remain blocked until the plan is upgraded or additional capacity is added.

Commercial note

Public plan pricing is shown above. Custom commercial terms, higher-volume capacity, and operational scope can be discussed via contact@adschecks.com.
