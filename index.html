Problem: ad slots are hard to verify at scale

Manual QA is slow, and screenshots without context are hard to audit. Teams need a consistent way to verify placement across locations and devices, with clear evidence of what was shown.

Opaque placement

Ad slots render differently by geography, device type, and layout variants.

Fragmented proof

Unstructured screenshots make it difficult to compare checks or track issues.

Operational drag

Manual verification consumes time and still leaves gaps in coverage.

What we check

Every check ties back to a specific slot, device, and location with a consistent report format.

Slot presence and visibility

  • Slot detected on page
  • Position and layout context
  • Visibility state (visible, partial, missing)

Geo and device coverage

  • Region-specific render checks
  • Desktop and mobile snapshots
  • Viewport and breakpoint metadata

Evidence bundle

  • Structured report output
  • Visual proof for each slot
  • Audit trail for review

Operational integrity

  • Repeatable checks
  • Clear issue flags
  • Export-ready structure
